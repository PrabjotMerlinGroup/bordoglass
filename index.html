Our profiles use advanced aluminum extrusion technology with reinforced corner joints and precision engineering. The 25mm slim profile incorporates internal strengthening ribs that provide superior structural integrity while maintaining an ultra-minimal aesthetic. This allows for larger glass panels without compromising on strength or safety.

Standard rectangular glass: 3 working days if ordered before 12pm. Custom shapes (circles, ovals): 5-10 working days. Complete door/partition systems: 7-14 working days. Custom RAL colors add 3-5 days. All times subject to stock availability.
