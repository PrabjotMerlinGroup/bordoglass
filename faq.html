Everything you need to know about our ultra-slim glass systems

Products

Our 25mm profiles use advanced aluminum extrusion technology with internal strengthening ribs and reinforced corner joints. The precision engineering and high-grade aluminum alloy provide superior structural integrity while maintaining an ultra-minimal aesthetic. This allows for larger glass panels without compromising strength or safety.

We offer a comprehensive range of glass thicknesses:

  • Float glass: 4mm, 6mm
  • Tempered glass: 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm
  • Laminated glass: 6.4mm, 8.8mm, 10.2mm
  • Double glazed units: 24mm, 28mm, 32mm total thickness

All glass conforms to BS EN safety standards.

25mm Slim: Our signature ultra-minimal profile, perfect for contemporary interiors where maximum transparency is desired.

30mm Standard: Balanced design with enhanced acoustic properties, ideal for office environments.

40mm Thermal: Features thermal break technology for improved insulation, perfect for external-facing partitions or energy-conscious projects.

Minimal maintenance keeps your system looking perfect:

  • Clean glass with standard glass cleaner monthly
  • Wipe aluminum frames with damp cloth
  • Lubricate hinges annually with silicone spray
  • Check and adjust soft-close mechanisms if needed
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners on all surfaces
