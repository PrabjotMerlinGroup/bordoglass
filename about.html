About Bordoglass

The UK's leading supplier of ultra-slim glass partition systems

Our Story

At Bordoglass, we believe that exceptional design begins with understanding the perfect balance between form and function. We have been at the forefront of developing innovative internal door and partition systems that transform spaces while maintaining the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Our flagship systems represent years of research and development, combining traditional aesthetic appeal with cutting-edge engineering. Inspired by classic Georgian steel doors, we've created modern solutions that bring together lightness, functionality, and timeless elegance.
