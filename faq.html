Products & Glass

Our signature 25mm profile is engineered using high-strength aluminium alloys that allow for minimal frame width while maintaining structural integrity. This gives you maximum glass area and light transmission compared to standard 35-50mm profiles from other manufacturers.

We offer clear float, tempered safety glass, laminated glass, frosted privacy glass, tinted glass (grey, bronze, blue), and mirror glass. Thicknesses range from 4mm to 12mm depending on application. All safety-critical applications use tempered or laminated glass to British Standards.

Yes, we can powder coat frames in any RAL colour. Standard colours (Jet Black RAL 9005, Anthracite Grey RAL 7016, Pure White RAL 9016) are held in stock for faster delivery. Custom colours add approximately 5 working days to lead times.

Tempered glass is heat-treated to be 5x stronger than standard glass and breaks into small, safer pieces. Laminated glass has a PVB interlayer that holds the glass together if broken, providing security and sound insulation. We recommend laminated for doors and areas requiring extra security.
