About Bordoglass

Pioneering excellence in internal doors and partition systems for over two decades

Our Story

At Bordoglass, we believe that exceptional design begins with understanding the perfect balance between form and function. Since our establishment, we have been at the forefront of developing innovative internal door and partition systems that transform spaces while maintaining the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Our flagship Xladeco system represents years of research and development, combining traditional aesthetic appeal with cutting-edge engineering. Inspired by classic Georgian steel doors, we've created a modern solution that brings together lightness, functionality, and timeless elegance.
